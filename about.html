About TNR Feline Friends



Who we are...

This group is dedicated in working in helping feral cats living healthier lives. This may be by completing trap, neuter or spay, and returning to back to where they were trapped. Some cats are able to be domesticated in which they will be paired with a foster and domesticated until ready to be adopted.
